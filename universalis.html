Other dates

The Universalis web site shows you:

  • Yesterday.
  • Today.
  • The week ahead.

This is possible through the kindness of the various copyright owners who have allowed us to reproduce their texts here. The one-week limit is a compromise between giving you free access and giving them some reward for their hard work.

If you want to see texts for more than a week ahead (or texts from the past), then you need to get one of our apps or programs.

We have apps for:

  • iPhone / iPad / iPod Touch.
  • Android (phones and tablets).
  • Amazon devices (Fire and Kindle Fire).

We have programs for:

  • Windows.
  • Mac.

You can also create e-books for:

  • Amazon Kindle.
  • Other e-book readers (reading the ePub format).

All these formats give you access to unlimited dates. They also give you the Grail psalms for the Liturgy of the Hours, and the option of the NAB texts for Mass if you are in the USA.
